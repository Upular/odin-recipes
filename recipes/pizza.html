Delicous tropical pizza recipe

Experiment with a vibrant twist on a culinary classic with this vibrant Tropical Pizza recipe. This dish brings a slice of paradise straight to your dinner table by layering sweet, juicy pineapple chunks and savory, smoky ham over a crispy, golden crust. A rich, tangy tomato sauce base ties the ingredients together, while a generous blanket of melted, bubbly mozzarella cheese ensures a perfect pull with every single bite. It is a harmonious balance of sweet and savory notes designed to elevate your standard pizza night.

Take your flavor journey a step further with fresh, modern upgrades to the traditional Hawaiian style. A scattered handful of crisp red onions, bright jalapeño slices, and a final drizzle of spicy hot honey add an unexpected kick of heat that cuts through the sweetness. Freshly torn basil leaves thrown on right after baking deliver a fragrant, clean finish. This quick, crowd-pleasing recipe transforms simple, accessible ingredients into an unforgettable, gourmet backyard vacation.

ingredients:

  • Pizza dough: 1 pre-made store-bought or homemade crust
  • Tomato sauce: ½ cup tangy pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese: 2 cups shredded, low-moisture cheese
  • Smoked ham: 1 cup diced or torn slices
  • Pineapple: 1 cup fresh or canned chunks, drained well
  • Red onion: ¼ cup thinly sliced
  • Jalapeño: 1 small pepper, thinly sliced into rounds
  • Hot honey: 1 to 2 tablespoons for drizzling
  • Fresh basil: A handful of torn leaves for a post-bake garnish

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 230°C
  2. Place a pizza stone inside if you use one.
  3. Pat the pineapple chunks dry with paper towels.
  4. Removing moisture prevents a soggy pizza crust.
  5. Roll out dough onto parchment paper.
  6. Spread tomato sauce evenly across the dough.
  7. Leave a half-inch border for the crust.
  8. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella over the sauce.
  9. Layer the smoked ham chunks on top.
  10. Scatter the dried pineapple and red onions.
  11. Add the jalapeño slices evenly across cheese.
  12. Slide the pizza into the preheated oven.
  13. Cook for 12 to 15 minutes total.
  14. Remove when cheese bubbles and crust browns.
  15. Drizzle hot honey over the hot pizza.
  16. Scatter fresh torn basil leaves on top.
  17. Cut into slices and serve immediately.
