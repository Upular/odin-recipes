Delicious Lasagna recipe

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this premier Classic Lasagna recipe, a staple of traditional Italian-American cuisine. This dish features layers of tender pasta sheets, a rich, slow-simmered meat sauce, and a creamy cheese filling. Every bite delivers a perfect balance of savory beef, tangy tomatoes, and melted, bubbly mozzarella cheese. It is a hearty, crowd-pleasing masterpiece designed to bring warmth to any family gathering. It is a harmonious balance of sweet and savory notes designed to elevate your standard pizza night.

Take this timeless recipe to the next level with a blend of ground beef and sweet Italian sausage. The addition of fresh herbs like basil and parsley enhances the deeply aromatic marinara base. Layered with a velvety ricotta cheese mixture and finished with a golden parmesan crust, this dish locks in moisture and flavor. This straightforward recipe transforms simple pantry staples into a spectacular, restaurant-quality center piece.

ingredients:

  • Pasta: 12 classic lasagna noodles, boiled until al dente
  • Meat: ½ pound ground beef and ½ pound sweet Italian sausage
  • Sauce: 36 ounces premium marinara sauce
  • Ricotta: 15 ounces whole-milk ricotta cheese
  • Egg: 1 large egg to bind the cheese filling
  • Mozzarella: 3 cups shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan: ½ cup finely grated parmesan cheese
  • Herbs: ¼ cup chopped fresh Italian parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C
  2. Cook beef and sausage in a large skillet.
  3. Remove excess grease from the browned meat.
  4. Stir marinara sauce into the meat and warm through.
  5. Combine ricotta, egg, and parsley in a bowl.
  6. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Arrange 4 cooked noodles over the base sauce.
  8. Layer one-third of the ricotta mixture over noodles.
  9. Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella cheese over ricotta.
  10. Repeat the noodle, ricotta, and mozzarella layers twice.
  11. Pour remaining meat sauce over the final layer of noodles.
  12. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and all parmesan on top.
  13. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  14. Uncover and bake for another 25 minutes until bubbly.
  15. Let stand for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
